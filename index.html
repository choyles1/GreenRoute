☁ Cloud Sync Setup
1

Create a free JSONBin account

Go to jsonbin.io and sign up for a free account. JSONBin stores your app data securely in the cloud so it syncs across all your devices.

Open jsonbin.io ↗
2

Create a new Bin

After logging in, click "Create Bin". In the editor, type {} and click Save. Copy the Bin ID from the URL or the info panel — it looks like: 64f3a1b2abc123def456

3

Get your API Key

In JSONBin, click your profile icon → "API Keys". Copy your Master Key (starts with $2a$10$). Keep this private — it gives full access to your bins.

4

Your company info (for printed documents)

Your data stays in your private JSONBin. Free tier supports up to 10,000 requests/month and 1MB per bin.
